# Business Requirements Document (BRD) - DNAV

## 1. Project Overview
The Decarbonized Net Asset Value (DNAV) Engine is designed to integrate carbon-weighted liabilities into traditional financial NAV calculations. This ensures that fund managers can price the "true fair value" of their portfolios in an April 2026 regulatory environment.

### 1.1 The Pain Points
- **Unquantified Liability:** Funds have no way to deduct Carbon Taxes (CBAM Final Regime) from their daily NAV.
- **Reporting Fragmentation:** Sustainability data is disconnected from financial ledgers (SAP, Bloomberg).
- **Greenwashing Risk:** Lack of audit trails for carbon intensity claims under **Reasonable Assurance** mandates.
- **Supply Chain Blindness:** Inability to comply with **SEBI FY26 Value Chain** disclosure standards.

## 2. Business Objectives
- **Carbon-Adjusted Pricing:** Enable real-time calculation of "Carbon-Adjusted NAV" (CANAV).
- **Compliance Readiness:** Align with SFDR Article 8/9 and PCAF Financed Emissions standards.
- **Audit-Ready Data:** Provide a clear "Data Lineage" from source (SAP/M365) to final output.

## 3. User Personas
- **Fund Accountant:** Needs to reconcile carbon liabilities with the general ledger.
- **Compliance Officer (GRC):** Needs to verify audit trails and "Fraud Sentry" alerts.
- **Portfolio Manager:** Needs to view "Temperature Alignment" gauges to stay within 1.5°C targets.
