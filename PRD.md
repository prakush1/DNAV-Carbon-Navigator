# PRD: DNAV (Decarbonized Net Asset Value)

## 🎯 1. Mission Statement
DNAV (Decarbonized Net Asset Value) is an audit-grade carbon-finance engine designed for the April 2026 regulatory landscape. It bridges the gap between traditional Private Equity accounting and the "Reasonable Assurance" requirements of SFDR 2.0 (EU) and BRSR Core (India).

---

## 🛠️ 2. The Problem vs. The DNAV Solution

| **Pain Point (The Problem)** | **DNAV Solution (The Feature)** | **Impact** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Manual Data Scavenging**: ESG data is stuck in PDF invoices, emails, and isolated SAP modules. | **Federated Ingest Hub**: Simulated connectors for SAP BTP, MS Graph (SharePoint), and IMAP (Email). | 80% reduction in manual data entry. |
| **"Greenwashing" Risk**: Companies reporting fake or "phantom" carbon offsets. | **AI Fraud & Anomaly Detection**: Real-time GRC logic identifying certificate re-use and phantom credits. | 100% audit-traceable data integrity. |
| **Static Reporting**: Carbon scores are calculated once a year, but NAV changes every day. | **Live Carbon-Adjusted NAV**: Real-time subtraction of carbon-liabilities from financial NAV. | Daily risk-adjusted portfolio visibility. |
| **Regulatory Misalignment**: Different rules for EU (SFDR), India (BRSR), and USA (SEC). | **Jurisdictional Logic Switch**: Dynamic rule-sets that update calculation logic based on the selected country's 2026 standards. | Instant compliance across 4 global regions. |
| **Audit Failures**: Auditors (PwC/EY) cannot verify how a number was calculated. | **Live Session Ledger (Audit Trail)**: Permanent, timestamped record of every action, manual override, and AI decision. | "Reasonable Assurance" ready from Day 1. |

---

## 🚀 3. Core Modules (MVP Scope)

1.  **Portfolio Carbon Navigator**: Visual dashboard showing Gross NAV vs. Carbon-Adjusted NAV.
2.  **Strategic Stress Tester**: Monte Carlo-style simulation of NAV if carbon prices hit $200/tonne.
3.  **AI GRC Oversight**: Tab for reviewing automated fraud alerts and compliance gaps.
4.  **Knowledge Hub**: Deep-dive glossary and interactive project documentation (BRD/FRD/SOP).

---

## 🗓️ 4. Roadmap (April 2026 & Beyond)
- **Q2 2026**: Integration with direct satellite-based methane monitoring for real-time Scope 1 audits.
- **Q3 2026**: Fully decentralized "Carbon Passports" based on Blockchain-STP for supply chain trust.
- **Q4 2026**: Predictive AI Forecasting for EU CBAM (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) tariffs.

---

## 🔒 5. Safety & Controls
- **AI Kill Switch**: Immediate manual override to disable autonomous logic if variance exceeds 5%.
- **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)**: All AI-detected fraud flags require manual "Analyst Clearance" before being logged to the official ledger.
